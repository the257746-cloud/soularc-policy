1. Information we collect

We collect the following information directly from users:

  • Email address (for account registration)
  • Username / display name
  • Authentication data (Firebase UID, login tokens)

We also collect derivative information via Firebase Analytics, including device data and app usage statistics.

2. How we use your information

  • Deliver and facilitate app services
  • Evaluate and improve our services, products, and user experience
  • Identify usage trends and performance issues
  • Protect our services and respond to user inquiries
  • Send administrative notifications

3. Disclosure to third parties

We share user information with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Cloud Firestore, Auth, Storage) – app infrastructure
  • Firebase Authentication / Google Sign-In – account management
  • Firebase Analytics – app analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics – performance monitoring
  • Google Cloud Platform / Firebase Hosting – cloud hosting

We have data processing agreements in place with these providers for GDPR compliance.

4. User accounts

  • Users can create accounts using email authentication.
  • To update or delete your account, log in to account settings or contact us at soularcstudios@gmail.com.

5. Data security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure communication.

6. Data retention

Personal information is retained as long as the account exists. Derivative data may be retained for app analytics and improvement.

7. Children’s privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified through in-app updates or on the store listing.

9. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at soularcstudios@gmail.com.
